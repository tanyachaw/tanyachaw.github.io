I've been lucky to work at some very cool places. Here's an informal resume.


The Washington Post (page)

I produced mostly South Asia and International audio stories for 'Post Reports' and also assisted on 'The 7'. Memorable pieces include one on the sinking of a submersible off the coast of Greece and the state of Press Freedom in India.


Radiolab

A WHIRLWIND! I pitched and failed and got advice and researched and interviewed and made a promo and took myself way too seriously and my voice made it in an episode and found myself at the New York Public Radio gala where I was 19 and terrified (pictured below).


Out There

I produced a story on a family friend about bone diseases, fear, and mountain biking.


The Daily Cal

I made my first ever audio piece asking people in San Francisco about the meaning of life. I also produced a story on People's Park that won that year's best podcast award from the CCMA.
