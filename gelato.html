Gelato

I've self-diagnosed myself as lactose intolerant, although I might actually be lactose sensitive because my symptoms aren't crazy. My stomach gets all bloated and I walk around in minor discomfort. Regardless, I ignore this lactose sensitivity for gelato.

This began in Berkeley. I just had dinner with a friend and was walking home. It was a great evening and I saw Caravaggio. I never planned on going in but I did, and got a hazelnut and pistachio number. I ate it on my way and it somehow changed my life. My stride slowed down. I looked at the houses with the yellow light and bookshelves inside and the trees around them. Something about buying gelato for yourself, like you are a child, is magic.

I had similar moments with gelato afterwards. In Manhattan, I had gelato after a work thing and just sat outside on a bench people watching. Then I had the best gelato of my life a few blocks away a couple weeks later. Then in Goa I saw Arshad Warsi at a gelato place, which again ended up being one of the best gelatos of my life. So I'm gonna map em.
