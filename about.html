I was raised between New Delhi and the Bay Area. In my adolescence, I was on the international red list for kidnapped children, but that’s a story for later (don’t worry, not tragic). During the pandemic, I started a kombucha business (the passion for which fizzled out after a while), an oatmeal business (the remains of which you can find here and which never broke even), and running (which I’m still trying to figure out). I am a second-year at UC Berkeley, and when I’m not studying, I dabble in many existential crises and make audio stories.

I deleted most of my social media a while ago but you can find me on linkedin or email me at tanyachawla at berkeley dot edu.
