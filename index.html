I was raised between New Delhi and the Bay Area. In my adolescence, I was actually on the international red list for kidnapped children, but that’s a story for later. During the pandemic, I started a kombucha business (the passion for which fizzled out after a while), an oatmeal business (which never broke even) , and running (which I’m still trying to figure out). I’m a second-year at UC Berkeley studying computer science, applied math, and linguistics, and when I’m not doing those three things, I enjoy making radio.
